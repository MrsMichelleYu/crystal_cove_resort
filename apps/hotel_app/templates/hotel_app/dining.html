Dining Options




Crystal Cove restaurants are carefully designed for a diverse dining experience in a variety of atmospheres. You'll enjoy a genuine culinary adventure in Aruba, provided by our world-class chefs and ranging from our gourmet menus to casual dining, and from a lobby lounge to a fun beach bar.



Sunset Grille Restaurant

Enjoy an unforgettable dining experience and breathtaking scenery at Sunset Grille, an energetic, modern steakhouse. Diners enjoy a variety of steaks, chops, and a selection of succulent seafood - all among awe-inspiring views.

HOURS

MONDAY - SUNDAY 8:30 am - 10:30 pm




Water's Edge Dining

Enjoy a romantic gourmet dinner by the beach with great views and the refreshing Caribbean breeze. Offering gourmet seafood, steaks, and pasta.

HOURS

MONDAY - SUNDAY 5:30 pm - 10:30 pm



Barefoot Beachside Bar
